2001/2006
After a couple of years of teaching myself how to play guitar and piano and listening to lots of Burt Bacharach, I decided to start writing my own songs. They were really just for the ears of friends and family but it did awaken something in me; I loved it.
2006/2011
At this point my songs, whilst still bedroom demos, started to become fuller and richer. I began to imagine them played by a band. Thankfully, I had a few friends who helped me do just that. I had a lot of fun and learned a lot.
2011/2016
For me, this was the period I wrote my best songs. It also coincided with playing in the best band I had ever been in and writing and performing with some other really talented musicians and singers.
